Privacy Policy

LinkedIn Job Filter & Highlighter

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect any data. Everything stays on your device. Your privacy is 100% protected.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data to external servers. All data processing happens locally on your device.

2. Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's secure storage API:

  • Job application data (job titles, company names, locations, dates applied, job URLs)
  • Your settings and preferences (highlight colors, filter options)
  • Company blacklist (companies you choose to hide)
  • Extension installation date (for statistics display)

This data never leaves your computer.

3. Data Usage

Your locally-stored data is used solely to:

  • Highlight jobs you've already applied to on LinkedIn job search pages
  • Display application statistics in the extension popup dashboard
  • Filter and hide jobs based on your preferences
  • Prevent duplicate job applications

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Ever. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no external API calls.

5. Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • Access to linkedin.com: To detect applied jobs and add visual highlights on LinkedIn pages
  • Storage: To save your application data and settings locally on your device
  • Tabs: To open LinkedIn's "My Jobs" page and refresh tabs when you change settings

We only access LinkedIn.com pages. We do not access any other websites or your browsing history.

6. Data Security

All data is stored using Chrome's encrypted storage API. Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of data interception or server breaches.

7. User Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Download your application data anytime (JSON or CSV format)
  • Delete: Clear all data by uninstalling the extension or using browser settings
  • Modify: Change settings, colors, and filters anytime

8. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. It is completely standalone and privacy-focused.

9. LinkedIn Integration

This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by LinkedIn Corporation. It simply enhances your job search experience by reading publicly visible job information on LinkedIn pages you visit.

We do not access your LinkedIn credentials, account information, messages, or connections.

10. Children's Privacy

This extension is designed for job seekers and is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
